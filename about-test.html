About

Senior Visual Designer

I am a multitask graphic designer with more than 15 years of experience. The past 10 years, I was managing my own design Canadian corporation. I developed long-term partnerships with clients by providing customer-focus solutions, strong design and organization skills, and efficiency managing simultaneous projects and deadlines.

Throughout the years I had the opportunity to create a wide variety of design projects (packaging, merchandising materials, brochures, catalogues, trade show displays, logos, mobile applications and web sites).

With a UI Design course recently completed, I am also fit to create users focus solutions, and apply technical constraints to designing for any kind of media and platform such as print, desktop and mobile applications (Android and iOS).
